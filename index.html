About Me

Certified Makeup Artist with extensive experience in bridal, fashion, editorial, and special effects makeup. Adept at airbrushing, HD makeup application, skincare consultation, and color theory to enhance natural beauty.

Skilled in client consultation, cosmetic application, contouring, highlighting, and facial aesthetics. Strong expertise in hygiene & sanitation standards, beauty trends, and product knowledge.

Passionate about delivering high-quality, customized beauty services for photoshoots, weddings, and special events. My creative approach turns makeup into art, pushing boundaries while maintaining precision and technique.

With a background in education and economics, I bring a unique perspective to the beauty industry, combining creativity with analytical thinking and meticulous attention to detail.

Based in Dubai, UAE, I work with clients both locally and internationally to create stunning looks tailored to their specific needs and preferences.
